<p><p>The stacked ceramic patch antenna market includes two main types: active antenna and passive antenna. Active antennas are equipped with amplifiers or other active components that enhance the signal strength and improve performance. These antennas can amplify weak signals and compensate for signal loss in the transmission process. On the other hand, passive antennas do not contain any active components and rely solely on the received electromagnetic waves to operate. They are simpler in design and generally used in applications where signal amplification is not required or is provided by external devices.</p></p>
