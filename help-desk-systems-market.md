<p><p>Help desk systems can be divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based help desk systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and easy accessibility from anywhere. On the other hand, on-premise help desk systems are installed and operated on local servers within an organization's premises. They provide greater control and security but require upfront hardware and software investments. Organizations should consider their specific needs and priorities when selecting between these two market types.</p></p>
