<p><p>Tensioners are devices used to apply tension to various materials, such as belts or chains, to prevent slack or slippage. There are three types of tensioners commonly found in the market. Manual tensioners require manual adjustment, making them suitable for smaller applications. Spring loaded tensioners automatically adjust to maintain tension, ideal for medium-sized systems. Floating tensioners effectively adapt to changes in tension caused by shocks or vibrations, making them perfect for larger and more dynamic systems. Each tensioner type serves a specific purpose in ensuring proper tension and preventing malfunction or damage.</p></p>
