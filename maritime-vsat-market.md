Different market types exist within Maritime VSAT, including Ku Band, C Band, L Band, and HTS Band. Ku Band offers high capacity and wider coverage, C Band provides reliable communication with good weather performance, L Band offers global coverage and is suitable for smaller vessels, and HTS Band utilizes high throughput satellites for faster and more efficient data transmission. Each market type caters to specific needs of the maritime industry in terms of coverage, capacity, and performance.</p></p>
