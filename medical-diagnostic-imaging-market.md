Ultrasound equipment uses sound waves to create images of organs and tissues. MRI equipment utilizes magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of internal structures. Nuclear imaging market involves techniques like PET and SPECT, which use radioactive materials to detect diseases and evaluate organ functionality. These imaging technologies play a critical role in medical diagnostics and aid in effective patient care.</p></p>
