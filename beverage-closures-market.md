<p><p>Beverage closures are essential for sealing and preserving the contents of beverage containers. There are two primary types of beverage closures: plastic and metal. Plastic beverage closures are commonly made from polyethylene or polypropylene and offer advantages like being lightweight, cost-effective, and easily customizable. Metal beverage closures, typically made from aluminum, are known for their durability, tamper-evidence, and ability to provide an airtight seal. Both types of closures play a significant role in maintaining the freshness and safety of beverages, catering to different packaging needs in the market.</p></p>
