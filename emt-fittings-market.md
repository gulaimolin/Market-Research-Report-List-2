<p><p>EMT fittings refer to the electrical metallic tubing fittings used in electrical installations. The market for EMT fittings encompasses various types, including screw fixed and spinning types. Screw fixed EMT fittings feature a threaded connection system, where the fitting screws onto the EMT. This type ensures a secure and robust connection. On the other hand, spinning EMT fittings provide a quick and easy installation process. They allow the EMT to spin after installation, enabling easier cable pulling and adjustments. Both types serve different needs in the electrical industry, catering to different installation requirements.</p></p>
