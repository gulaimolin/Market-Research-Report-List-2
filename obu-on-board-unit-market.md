<p><p>OBU (On Board Unit) refers to a device used in electronic toll collection systems. This version is typically used in areas where smart cards are not required or popular. On the other hand, the double-chip OBU market includes OBUs with an IC card interface, enabling users to make payments using a smart card. This type is commonly used in regions where smart card technology is prevalent for toll collection purposes.</p></p>
