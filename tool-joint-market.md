<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>There are three types of tool joint markets that are commonly found in the oil and gas industry. The first is the Threaded Screw-on Type, where the tool joint is connected to the drill pipe by screwing it on. The second is the Counter-bore Weld Type, where the tool joint is welded onto the drill pipe with a recessed area for the weld. The third is the Flash or Inertia Weld Type, where a specialized welding process is used to fuse the tool joint to the drill pipe. Each of these types has its own advantages and applications.</p></p>
