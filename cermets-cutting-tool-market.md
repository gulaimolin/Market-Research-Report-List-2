<p><p>Cermets cutting tools are utilized in various industries for precision cutting and machining applications. The market for these tools can be divided into two types: silicon nitride ceramics and boride silicon ceramics. Silicon nitride ceramics provide high wear resistance, excellent thermal shock resistance, and improved toughness, making them ideal for high-speed cutting processes. On the other hand, boride silicon ceramics are known for their exceptional hardness and resistance to deformation, making them suitable for cutting hard materials. Both types play a vital role in enhancing cutting efficiency and achieving optimal results in different industries.</p></p>
