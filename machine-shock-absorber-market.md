<p><p>The machine shock absorber market consists of two major types: plastic and metal. Plastic shock absorbers are made of resilient materials like polyurethane, rubber, or thermoplastic elastomers. They provide effective damping and vibration isolation properties, making them suitable for lighter loads and lower impact forces. Metal shock absorbers, on the other hand, are constructed from alloys like steel, aluminum, or titanium. They offer superior strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications involving higher loads and intense impacts. Both types cater to different needs based on the specific requirements of the machines and their operating conditions.</p></p>
