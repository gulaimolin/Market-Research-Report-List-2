<p><p>A brushless impact wrench is a power tool commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. It is designed to deliver high torque output with minimum effort from the user. Unlike traditional impact wrenches, brushless impact wrenches do not have brushes that come in contact with the commutator. Instead, they use an electronic control system to deliver power to the motor, resulting in better efficiency, longer lifespan, and improved performance.</p><p>The market for brushless impact wrenches has been witnessing significant growth in recent years.
