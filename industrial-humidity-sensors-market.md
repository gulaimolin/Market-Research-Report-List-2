<p><p>The Industrial Humidity Sensors market consists of two main types: Relative Humidity Sensors and Absolute Humidity Sensors. Relative Humidity Sensors measure the amount of moisture present in the air relative to the maximum amount the air can hold at that temperature. They are widely used in industries such as HVAC, agriculture, and food processing. On the other hand, Absolute Humidity Sensors directly measure the exact amount of moisture present in the air regardless of the temperature. They find applications in pharmaceutical, automotive, and aerospace industries where precise humidity control is crucial.</p></p>
