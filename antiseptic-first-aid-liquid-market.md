<p><p>Antiseptic first aid liquid market types include alcohols and aldehydes, amides and biguanides, quaternary ammonium compounds, and others. Alcohols and aldehydes act as disinfectants by denaturing proteins and disrupting cell membranes. Amides and biguanides, like chlorhexidine, have broad-spectrum antimicrobial properties and are effective against bacteria and fungi. Quaternary ammonium compounds, including benzalkonium chloride, kill microorganisms by disrupting their cell membranes. The "others" category may include antiseptic agents such as iodine, hydrogen peroxide, and silver-based compounds, each with their respective mechanisms of action against pathogens.</p></p>
