<p><p>Web tension sensors are devices used in the manufacturing industry to measure the tension or force applied to webs (continuous materials, such as paper or fabric) during production processes. There are different types of web tension sensors available, including pillow block or pillow block base sensors, and through-hole or stud mount sensors. Pillow block or pillow block base sensors are mounted on a pillow block or base unit, while through-hole or stud mount sensors are installed directly through a hole in the machine frame or mounted using studs. Both types serve the purpose of accurately measuring web tension during production.</p></p>
