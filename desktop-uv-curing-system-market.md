<p><p>Desktop UV curing systems are devices used for curing or drying ultraviolet (UV) adhesives and coatings on various materials. There are two types of systems available in the market: spot-type and line-type. Spot-type systems emit a concentrated UV light beam for curing small areas accurately and quickly. They are often used for detailed applications or repairs. On the other hand, line-type systems emit a wider UV light beam, covering a larger area for continuous curing. They are commonly used for industrial production purposes or larger-scale projects.</p></p>
