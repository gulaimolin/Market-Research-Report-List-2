<p><p>The mushroom valve market encompasses three types: 2-way, 3-way, and 4-way valves. In a 2-way market, the valve has two ports, allowing for the control of flow or blockage of fluid in a single direction. A 3-way market features three ports, enabling the diversion of fluid between two different directions. Lastly, a 4-way market involves four ports, allowing for the control of flow or diversion of fluid between two separate paths. These different types cater to various applications and requirements across industries such as automotive, pharmaceutical, and oil and gas.</p></p>
