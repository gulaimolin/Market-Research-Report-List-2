<p><p>The 3-phase distribution boards market consists of different types, including main distribution boards and emergency distribution boards. Main distribution boards are the primary source of electrical power distribution in a building or facility. They receive power from the main power feed and distribute it to various sub-panels and circuits. On the other hand, emergency distribution boards are designed to handle critical situations and provide backup power during emergencies by connecting to generators or uninterruptible power supplies. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient power distribution within a building or facility.</p></p>
