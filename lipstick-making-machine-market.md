<p><p>The lipstick making machine market consists of two main types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic machines. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention for some tasks, such as pouring ingredients and adjusting settings, while also automating certain processes. Fully-automatic machines, on the other hand, are designed to automate the entire lipstick manufacturing process, requiring minimal human involvement. These machines can handle tasks such as measuring ingredients, mixing, pouring, molding, and packaging without the need for constant supervision. Both types cater to different production needs and budgets in the lipstick making industry.</p></p>
