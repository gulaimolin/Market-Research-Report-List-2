<p><p>The Animal Vaccination Market includes different types of vaccines. Live attenuated vaccines are made from weakened forms of the disease-causing organism, stimulating a strong immune response. Inactivated vaccines contain killed pathogens, triggering an immune response without causing the disease. Other vaccines may use subunit vaccines, DNA vaccines, or recombinant vaccines. These alternatives use specific components or genetic material to elicit an immune response. These various types of vaccines provide options for preventing and controlling diseases in animals, helping to ensure their health and well-being.</p></p>
