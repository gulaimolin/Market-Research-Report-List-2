<p><p>Steam safety valve market types can be broadly categorized into low-pressure steam valves and high-pressure steam valves. Low-pressure steam valves are designed to handle steam at pressures below 15 psi, suitable for applications such as heating systems and small-scale steam processes. On the other hand, high-pressure steam valves are specifically designed to handle steam at pressures exceeding 15 psi, typically used in industrial facilities and power plants for more demanding steam applications. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining safety by releasing excess pressure and preventing equipment damage or failures.</p></p>
