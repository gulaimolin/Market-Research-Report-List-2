<p><p>Photointerrupters are sensor devices widely used in various industries for detecting the presence or absence of an object. Transmissive type photointerrupters consist of an infrared LED and a phototransistor placed opposite to each other, where interruption of the infrared beam triggers the sensor. Reflective type photointerrupters have a built-in reflector that reflects an emitted infrared beam, and presence or absence of an object can be detected based on the reflected beam. Both market types find applications in industrial automation, robotics, printing, and consumer electronics, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in object detection processes.</p></p>
