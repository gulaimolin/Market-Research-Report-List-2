<p><p>Runtime Application Self-protection (RASP) security is a type of cybersecurity technology that is designed to protect applications during runtime. It works by embedding security measures directly into the application code, allowing it to monitor and defend against threats in real-time. RASP technology provides continuous protection by identifying and mitigating vulnerabilities within the application itself, rather than relying solely on network infrastructure or perimeter defenses.</p><p>The Runtime Application Self-protection (RASP) security market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. Factors contributing to this growth include the increasing frequency and complexity of cyber threats, the rising adoption of cloud-based applications, and the growing demand for security solutions that can protect applications throughout their lifecycle. Additionally, the rapid digitalization of businesses and the increasing reliance on software applications are driving the need for enhanced application security.</p><p>The market is also witnessing various trends that are shaping its trajectory. This trend is expected to drive the adoption of RASP security solutions in various industries.</p><p>Overall, the Runtime Application Self-protection (RASP) security market is poised for substantial growth, with a projected CAGR of 11.4% during the forecast period.
