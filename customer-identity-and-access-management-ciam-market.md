The market is driven by factors such as rising instances of identity thefts and data breaches, stringent regulatory requirements regarding customer data protection, and the increasing demand for personalized customer experiences. Furthermore, the proliferation of smartphones, the growth of social media, and the increasing integration of various digital channels have also contributed to market growth.</p><p>The market is characterized by the emergence of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), and biometrics, which are being incorporated into CIAM solutions to enhance security measures and improve user experiences.
