<p><p>Lateral Flow Assay is a type of diagnostic test that detects the presence of specific substances in a sample. The market for Lateral Flow Assay can be classified into two types: Sandwich Assays and Competitive Assays. Sandwich Assays use two antibodies to capture and detect the target substance, while Competitive Assays involve competition between the target substance and a labeled substance for binding sites on a single antibody. These assays are widely used in various applications such as healthcare, food safety, and environmental monitoring for quick and reliable results.</p></p>
