<p><strong>The Data Fiber Optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Fiber Optic Cable market consists of two types: Single Mode and Multimode. Single Mode cables have a smaller core and allow only one mode of light to travel through them, resulting in longer transmission distances but requiring more expensive equipment. On the other hand, Multimode cables have a larger core that allows multiple light modes to pass through simultaneously, making them suitable for shorter transmission distances while being more cost-effective. Both Single Mode and Multimode cables play a crucial role in facilitating data transmission through the use of light signals.</p></p>
