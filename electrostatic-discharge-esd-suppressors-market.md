<p><p>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Suppressors are devices used to protect electronic components from damage caused by electrostatic discharge. Diode array suppressors consist of multiple diodes connected in a series, effectively diverting excessive electrical energy away from sensitive components. Polymer suppressors utilize polymer materials that can quickly absorb and dissipate electrostatic energy. Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diodes provide a fast response time to transient voltage spikes, diverting the excess energy and protecting the components. These suppressors ensure the safety and reliability of electronic devices by preventing potential damage from electrostatic discharge.</p></p>
