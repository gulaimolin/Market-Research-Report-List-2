<p><p>The hernia repair devices market includes two major types of products: hernia repair mesh products and hernia repair fixation devices. Hernia repair mesh products are used to reinforce weak tissues and support the damaged area, preventing future hernias. They are typically made of synthetic materials and can be absorbable or non-absorbable. On the other hand, hernia repair fixation devices are used to secure the mesh in place during the surgical procedure. They can be in the form of sutures, tacks, or other devices that help fixate the mesh securely.</p></p>
