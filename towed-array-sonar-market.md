<p><p>Towed Array Sonar is a technology used in underwater applications to detect and track objects, such as submarines. The market for Towed Array Sonar can be categorized into Active Towed Array Sonar and Passive Towed Array Sonar. Active Towed Array Sonar involves emitting sound waves into the water and capturing the echoes to locate objects. On the other hand, Passive Towed Array Sonar relies on listening for sound signals emitted by objects in the water, without emitting any sound itself. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various naval, commercial, and scientific applications.</p></p>
