<p><p>A monocular telescope, also known as a single-eye telescope, is a handheld device that allows users to view distant objects with higher magnification and clarity using only one eye. Monocular telescopes are widely used for activities such as bird watching, hunting, stargazing, and sports events.</p><p>The future outlook of the monocular telescope market is expected to be promising.
