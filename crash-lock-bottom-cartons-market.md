<p><p>Crash lock bottom cartons, also known as 1-2-3 bottom cartons, are widely used in packaging industries. They come in two market types: folded and glued. In the folded market, the cartons are supplied flat and need to be manually folded and assembled before use. This type requires minimal machinery and is suitable for smaller-scale operations. In the glued market, the cartons are pre-glued and supplied in their fully assembled form. It saves time and effort during packaging processes, making it ideal for high-volume production lines. Both market types offer sturdy and reliable packaging solutions.</p></p>
