<p><strong>The Direct-Mount Valve Manifolds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Way Mainfold Valves</li><li>Three Way Mainfold Valves</li><li>Five Way Mainfold Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The direct-mount valve manifolds market includes three types: two-way manifold valves, three-way manifold valves, and five-way manifold valves. Two-way manifold valves have two ports (inlet and outlet) and are commonly used in applications where simple on/off control is needed. Three-way manifold valves have three ports and are used for diverting flow or mixing fluids. Five-way manifold valves have five ports and are used for complex control and routing of fluids in systems with multiple functions or operations. These different types cater to a wide range of industrial requirements for control and regulation of fluid flows.</p></p>
