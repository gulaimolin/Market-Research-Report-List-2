<p><p>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) on plastics refers to a process of depositing thin films on plastic surfaces. There are different types of PVD techniques utilized in this market. Deposition PVD involves evaporating a solid material and condensing it onto the plastic surface. Sputtering PVD involves bombarding the surface with ionized particles to deposit the thin film. Pulsed Laser Deposition (PLD) utilizes laser ablation to evaporate the target material and deposit it onto the plastic surface. These techniques are commonly used in industries such as electronics, optics, and automotive for various applications.</p></p>
