<p><p>COD (Chemical Oxygen Demand) sensors are used to measure the concentration of organic pollutants in wastewater. There are two types of COD sensors available in the market: Digital and Analog. </p><p>Digital COD sensors are equipped with microprocessors that convert the measured data into digital signal, providing accurate and precise readings. They also offer features like real-time data logging and easy integration with other digital devices.</p><p>Analog COD sensors, on the other hand, provide voltage output proportional to the COD concentration. They are simpler in design and less expensive, but may not offer the same level of accuracy and advanced functionalities as digital sensors.</p><p>Both types of sensors have their own merits and are chosen based on specific requirements and budget considerations.</p></p>
