<p><p>The heavy-duty wheelchair market consists of two main types: manual heavy-duty wheelchairs and electric heavy-duty wheelchairs. Manual heavy-duty wheelchairs are designed to be self-propelled by the user or pushed by a caregiver. These wheelchairs are usually made of durable materials and have reinforced frames to support individuals with higher weight capacities. On the other hand, electric heavy-duty wheelchairs are powered by batteries and designed for individuals who may have difficulty self-propelling or require greater assistance. These wheelchairs offer motorized propulsion and often come with additional features such as adjustable seating and improved maneuverability.</p></p>
