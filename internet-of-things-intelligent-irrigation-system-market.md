<p><p>The Internet of Things (IoT) Intelligent Irrigation System market includes two types of controllers: Weather-based Controllers and Sensor-based Controllers. Weather-based Controllers rely on real-time weather data to determine the optimal irrigation schedule, ensuring that irrigation is only conducted when necessary. On the other hand, Sensor-based Controllers use various sensors to gather information about soil moisture, temperature, and other factors. This data helps determine the precise irrigation needs of plants, enabling more efficient water usage and preventing over or under watering. Both types of controllers improve irrigation efficiency and reduce water wastage.</p></p>
