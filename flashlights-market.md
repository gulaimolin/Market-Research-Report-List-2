<p><p>The flashlight market can be divided into two types: rechargeable and non-rechargeable flashlights. Rechargeable flashlights have built-in batteries that can be recharged using a power source, such as a USB or a wall outlet. They provide a more sustainable and cost-effective solution as they can be used multiple times without the need to constantly purchase batteries. On the other hand, non-rechargeable flashlights use disposable batteries which need to be replaced once they run out of power. While these flashlights offer convenience, they can be more expensive in the long run due to the ongoing battery replacements.</p></p>
