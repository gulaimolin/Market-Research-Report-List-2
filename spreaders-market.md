Electric spreaders use an electric motor to power the spreading mechanism, making them suitable for small to medium-scale applications. Electro-hydraulic spreaders utilize both electrical and hydraulic systems to provide higher lifting capacities and increased spreading force. They are commonly used in heavy-duty industries. Pneumatic spreaders are powered by compressed air and are ideal for specialized applications, such as handling delicate or sensitive materials. Each type of spreader offers unique advantages and is selected based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
