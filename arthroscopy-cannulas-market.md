<p><p>Arthroscopy cannulas are medical devices used during arthroscopic procedures to facilitate fluid flow and instrument insertion. There are two types of arthroscopy cannulas: reusable and disposable. Reusable cannulas are designed to be sterilized and reused multiple times, making them a cost-effective option in the long run. On the other hand, disposable cannulas are used for single procedures and then discarded, offering convenience and minimizing the risk of cross-contamination. Both types serve the purpose of providing a safe and effective means of accessing the joint during arthroscopic surgeries.</p></p>
