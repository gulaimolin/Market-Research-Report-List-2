<p><strong>The Air Blasting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Suction Type</li><li>Direct Pressure Type</li><li>Blower Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air blasting machine market consists of three types: gravity suction type, direct pressure type, and blower type. Gravity suction type machines utilize the principle of suction to create a vacuum that pulls the abrasive material into the blasting gun. Direct pressure type machines use compressed air to push the abrasive material out of the gun. Lastly, blower type machines rely on a blower to propel the abrasive material towards the surface being blasted. These different types cater to various industrial needs and provide different levels of efficiency and effectiveness in blasting processes.</p></p>
