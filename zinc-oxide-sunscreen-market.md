<p><p>The Zinc Oxide Sunscreen market is divided into two major types - Transparent Zinc Oxide and Non-nano Zinc Oxide. Transparent Zinc Oxide refers to sunscreens that contain micronized particles of zinc oxide, which are nearly colorless when applied on the skin. This type of sunscreen provides effective protection against both UVA and UVB rays without leaving a white residue. On the other hand, Non-nano Zinc Oxide sunscreens contain larger particles of zinc oxide, which are unable to penetrate the skin. This type of sunscreen is preferred by individuals concerned about the potential risks associated with nanoparticles.</p></p>
