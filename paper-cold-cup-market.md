Double-walled cups have an additional layer that provides better insulation, making them suitable for longer use. Triple-walled cups have three layers, offering the highest level of insulation, ideal for keeping drinks cold for an extended period. These cup types cater to different needs based on the desired duration of drink insulation.</p></p>
