<p><p>The market for vehicles designed for disabled individuals can be categorized into two types: adaptive four-wheelers and mobility scooters. Adaptive four-wheelers are modified versions of regular cars or trucks with customized features such as ramps, lifts, hand controls, and wheelchair-accessible modifications. These vehicles are suitable for individuals who have limited mobility but can still operate a regular vehicle. Mobility scooters, on the other hand, are electric-powered scooters specifically designed for individuals with mobility challenges. These scooters provide easy maneuverability and are ideal for short-distance travel within indoor or outdoor environments.</p></p>
