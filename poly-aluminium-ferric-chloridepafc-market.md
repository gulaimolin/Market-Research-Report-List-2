<p><p>Poly Aluminium Ferric Chloride (PAFC) is a chemical compound used in various industries, including water treatment and wastewater treatment. There are four types of PAFC market methods: Aluminum Method, Active Aluminum Hydroxide Method, Aluminum Oxide Method, and Aluminum Chloride Method. </p><p>In the Aluminum Method, aluminum is added to the reaction to produce PAFC. The Active Aluminum Hydroxide Method involves using active aluminum hydroxide as a raw material to synthesize PAFC. The Aluminum Oxide Method utilizes aluminum oxide as a catalyst to generate PAFC. Lastly, the Aluminum Chloride Method involves reacting aluminum chloride with iron chloride to produce PAFC. Each market method has its specific applications and benefits in different industrial processes.</p></p>
