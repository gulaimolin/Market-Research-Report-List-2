<p><p>The Steam Exhaust Muffler market consists of two types: the Outlet Steam Exhaust Muffler and the Inlet Steam Exhaust Muffler. The Outlet Steam Exhaust Muffler is designed to reduce noise levels and minimize the impact of steam exhaust during its discharge from various industrial processes. On the other hand, the Inlet Steam Exhaust Muffler is responsible for reducing noise and attenuating steam pressure during steam intake. Both types serve crucial roles in controlling noise pollution and optimizing steam exhaust processes in industries.</p></p>
