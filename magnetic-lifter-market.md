<p><p>The Magnetic Lifter market consists of two types: Manual and Automatic. Manual Magnetic Lifters are operated by human force and require direct contact with the lifter, providing users with more control and flexibility. These lifters are typically used for lighter loads and are cost-effective for small-scale operations. On the other hand, Automatic Magnetic Lifters are powered by electricity and operate without physical contact. They offer greater efficiency and productivity, making them suitable for heavier loads and larger industrial applications. These lifters are generally more expensive but provide enhanced convenience and safety features.</p></p>
