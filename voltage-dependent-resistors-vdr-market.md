<p><p>Voltage Dependent Resistors (VDRs) are electronic components that change their resistance value in response to changes in voltage. They are primarily used as protection devices in electrical circuits to suppress high voltage surges and limit transient current flow. VDRs are available in two different market types - SMD Type and Leaded Type. SMD Type VDRs are surface mount devices that are directly mounted on the circuit board, offering space-saving advantages. Leaded Type VDRs, on the other hand, have leads attached and are suitable for through-hole mounting on PCBs. Both types cater to diverse market needs and play a vital role in safeguarding electronic equipment.</p></p>
