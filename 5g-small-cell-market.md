<p><p>A 5G small cell is a low-powered wireless access point that facilitates high-speed data transmission in areas where network coverage is limited. These cells are deployed in dense urban areas, commercial buildings, stadiums, and other locations where demand for high-speed and high-bandwidth connectivity is high. They supplement the existing macrocell network and enable the delivery of high-capacity, low-latency communications required for applications like augmented reality, virtual reality, and Internet of Things (IoT).</p><p>The 5G small cell market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for high-speed and reliable network connectivity. The market is driven by factors such as the rising adoption of 5G technology, advancements in communication infrastructure, and the increasing number of internet users and connected devices.
