<p><ul><li>Public</li><li>Private & Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The enterprise communication infrastructure market consists of three main types: public, private, and hybrid. In a public market, the communication infrastructure is owned and operated by a third-party provider accessible to multiple organizations. In contrast, a private market involves a dedicated infrastructure solely owned and managed by an individual organization. Lastly, a hybrid market combines aspects of both public and private, allowing organizations to utilize a combination of shared and dedicated infrastructure based on their specific needs. Each market type offers different levels of control, security, and customization options for enterprise communication needs.</p></p>
