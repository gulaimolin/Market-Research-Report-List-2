<p><p>In the dynamic pricing software market, there are two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software refers to systems that are installed and operated on the customer's own servers and infrastructure. This type allows for greater control and customization but requires the customer to manage maintenance and updates. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet. This type offers greater flexibility, scalability, and easier maintenance as updates are handled by the vendor.</p></p>
