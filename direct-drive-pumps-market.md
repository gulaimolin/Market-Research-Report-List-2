<p><p>The direct drive pumps market is categorized into three types: centrifugal pumps, positive displacement pumps, and others. Centrifugal pumps use rotating impellers to increase the fluid's velocity and create pressure. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, transfer a fixed volume of fluid per rotation using pistons, gears, or lobes. The "others" category includes various pump designs like axial flow and regenerative turbine pumps that function based on different principles but serve similar purposes. These different types cater to various applications and customers' specific pumping requirements.</p></p>
