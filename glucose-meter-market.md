<p><ul><li>Glucose Oxidase Method</li><li>Glucose Dehydrogenase Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glucose Meter Market can be divided into two main types: the Glucose Oxidase Method and the Glucose Dehydrogenase Method. The Glucose Oxidase Method measures glucose levels by utilizing an enzyme that reacts with oxygen, producing a color change that is then measured. On the other hand, the Glucose Dehydrogenase Method measures glucose levels by using an enzyme that causes a chemical reaction, resulting in a current that is measured. Both methods are commonly used in glucose meters to provide accurate and quick results for glucose monitoring.</p></p>
