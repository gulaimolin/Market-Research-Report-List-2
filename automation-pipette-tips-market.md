<p><ul><li>Non-Filtered Pipette Tips</li><li>Filtered Pipette Tips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automation pipette tips market includes two main types: non-filtered pipette tips and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are designed for general use and do not contain any filtration system. They are suitable for simple liquid handling tasks. On the other hand, filtered pipette tips are equipped with a built-in filtration system to prevent aerosol contamination and protect the sample from airborne particles. These tips are commonly used in molecular biology and biosafety applications where sample purity is crucial.</p></p>
