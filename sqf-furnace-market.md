<p><p>The SQF furnace market is comprised of two main types: the Straight Through Type and the In-Out Type. The Straight Through Type refers to furnaces where the gas flow enters from one end of the furnace and exits from the other end in a straight line. This type is commonly used in industries with limited space. On the other hand, the In-Out Type furnace has the gas flow entering and exiting from the same end, making it suitable for larger industries with more space availability. Both types serve specific needs and are chosen based on spatial requirements in industrial settings.</p></p>
