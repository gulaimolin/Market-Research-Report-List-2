<p><p>Logic Output Photocouplers are electronic devices that consist of a light-emitting diode (LED) and a phototransistor or a photodarlington sensor for optical isolation. These photocouplers are widely used in various applications for isolating high voltage circuits from low voltage control circuits. </p><p>AC Logic Output Photocouplers are designed for alternating current applications. They provide high-speed response and high voltage isolation, making them suitable for applications that involve AC signals.</p><p>DC Logic Output Photocouplers, on the other hand, are designed for direct current applications. They offer low input-to-output propagation delay and high common-mode transient immunity, making them ideal for DC signal transmission.</p><p>Logic Inverter Market refers to the market for photocouplers that function as logic inverters. These devices have inputs and outputs that are compatible with digital logic circuits and are used to invert or change the logic level of a signal.</p><p>All three types of Logic Output Photocouplers are essential components in the electronics industry, providing efficient and reliable optical isolation for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
