<p><p>Biomass fuel testing market encompasses various types of testing, including calorific value, ash content, moisture content, sulphur content, and pH. Calorific value testing measures the energy content of the fuel, allowing for efficient utilization. Ash content testing determines the amount of minerals and impurities in the fuel, affecting its combustion quality. Moisture content testing assesses the water content in the fuel, impacting its energy potential. Sulphur content testing quantifies the amount of sulphur, as high levels can be harmful when burned. Lastly, pH testing measures the acidity or alkalinity of the fuel, which can influence its combustion stability.</p></p>
