A2 fire rated panels offer the highest level of fire resistance, while B, C, D, E, and F rated panels provide varying levels of fire protection. These ratings signify the duration for which the panels can withstand fire before losing their structural integrity. These panels are crucial in preventing the spread of fire and protecting lives and property.</p></p>
