<p><p>The airborne sensor market can be divided into two types: non-scanning and scanning. Non-scanning sensors capture data in a continuous manner as the aircraft moves along its path. They are typically used for applications like aerial imaging and surveillance. On the other hand, scanning sensors capture data by scanning the environment in a systematic pattern. They are often employed for tasks such as terrain mapping and geological surveys. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in gathering valuable information from the air, albeit using different methodologies.</p></p>
