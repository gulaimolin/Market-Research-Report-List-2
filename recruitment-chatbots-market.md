This integration enables chatbots to have access to candidate profiles, application status, and other relevant information, thereby providing a personalized and efficient experience to candidates.</p><p>Another trend is the emergence of voice-enabled chatbots. With the rapid advancement in voice recognition technology, chatbots are now equipped to understand and respond to voice commands.
