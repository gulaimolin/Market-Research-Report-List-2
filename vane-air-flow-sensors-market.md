<p><p>Vane air flow sensors are used to measure and monitor the flow of air in various applications. There are several types of vane air flow sensors available in the market. </p><p>1. Blade type vane air flow sensors use a rotating blade to determine the air velocity. The blade rotates according to the air flow, and the sensor measures this rotation to calculate the air speed.</p><p>2. Damper type vane air flow sensors use a hinged flap or damper that moves in response to the air flow. The sensor measures the movement of the damper to determine the air speed.</p><p>3. Other types of vane air flow sensors include multi-vane sensors that use multiple vanes to enhance accuracy and hot wire anemometers that use a heated wire to measure the cooling effect of air flow. These sensors provide additional features and capabilities for specific applications.</p></p>
