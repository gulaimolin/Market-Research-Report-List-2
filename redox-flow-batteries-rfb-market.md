<p><p>Redox Flow Batteries (RFB) are a type of rechargeable batteries that store and release electrical energy by using redox reactions. The market for RFBs can be classified into two major types: Vanadium Redox Flow Battery and Hybrid Flow Battery. Vanadium Redox Flow Battery technology uses vanadium electrolyte on both sides of the cell, providing high energy density and long cycle life. On the other hand, Hybrid Flow Batteries combine different chemistries, often vanadium and organic molecules, to offer enhanced performance and cost-effectiveness in energy storage applications.</p></p>
