The market for these sensors can be categorized into two types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensors and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) Sensors. </p><p>SAW sensors use an acoustic wave that propagates along the surface of a material, allowing for highly sensitive and accurate measurements. BAW sensors, on the other hand, use waves that propagate through the bulk of a material, offering higher performance in terms of frequency range and power handling capabilities. Both types find applications in industries like automotive, healthcare, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
