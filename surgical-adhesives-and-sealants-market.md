<p><p>Surgical adhesives and sealants are substances used in medical procedures to close or seal wounds, incisions, or surgical sites. There are several types available in the market. Fibrin sealants are made from human blood proteins and help promote blood clotting. Collagen-based adhesives are derived from animal sources and provide structural support. Gelatin-based adhesives are derived from collagen and offer strong adhesion. Cyanoacrylates are fast-acting adhesives that bond tissues quickly. Polymeric hydrogels are water-based and offer flexibility and a protective barrier. Urethane-based adhesives provide strength and durability.</p></p>
