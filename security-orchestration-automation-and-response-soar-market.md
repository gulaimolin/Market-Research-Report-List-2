<p><p>Security Orchestration Automation and Response (SOAR) refers to a comprehensive solution that combines security orchestration, automation, and incident response capabilities to streamline and enhance an organization's security operations. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period.</p><p>One of the major trends in the SOAR market is the adoption of machine learning and artificial intelligence (AI) technologies. These technologies enable organizations to analyze vast amounts of security data in real-time, identify patterns, and automate repetitive tasks, thereby reducing human intervention and accelerating incident response.</p><p>Another trend is the integration of SOAR platforms with threat intelligence and vulnerability management solutions. By integrating these tools, organizations can gather real-time threat intelligence data, correlate it with existing security information, and automate the remediation process, resulting in improved threat detection and response capabilities.</p><p>Additionally, the market is witnessing the emergence of managed security service providers (MSSPs) offering SOAR-as-a-Service.
