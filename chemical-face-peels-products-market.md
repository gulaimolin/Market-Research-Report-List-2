<p><p>Chemical face peels are skincare products used to exfoliate and rejuvenate the skin. There are several types of chemical face peels available in the market. Alpha Hydroxy Acid (AHA) peels work by removing the top layer of dead skin cells, promoting a brighter complexion. Beta Hydroxy Acid (BHA) peels penetrate deeper into the pores and are effective for treating acne-prone skin. Retinoic Acid peels are derived from Vitamin A and are used to reduce wrinkles and improve skin texture. Phenol Croton Oil peels are the strongest and can treat deep wrinkles and scars. Other types of peels include enzyme peels and combination peels.</p></p>
