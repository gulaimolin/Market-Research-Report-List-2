<p><p>Radiant tube heaters are classified into various types that cater to different heating needs. U-type radiant tube heaters are designed in a U-shape, allowing for even heat distribution and efficient fuel consumption. Line type radiant tube heaters consist of a long, straight tube that radiates heat in a linear pattern, suiting applications with narrow spaces. Other types refer to radiant tube heaters with unique designs or features, such as low-intensity heaters, high-intensity heaters, vacuum radiant heaters, or custom-made units. Each type offers specific advantages and suitability for different environments and requirements.</p></p>
