<p><p>Digital veterinary thermometers are designed to accurately measure the body temperature of animals. There are two types of digital veterinary thermometers available in the market, namely anal type and ear type. Anal type thermometers are inserted into the animal's rectum to measure the temperature internally. On the other hand, ear type thermometers use infrared technology to measure the temperature through the animal's ear canal. Both types ensure quick and accurate temperature readings, helping veterinarians to monitor and diagnose animals' health conditions effectively.</p></p>
