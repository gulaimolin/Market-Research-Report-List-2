<p><p>Food Natural Vanillin is a market segment that includes two types: Eugenol Synthesis and Ferulic Acid Synthesis. Eugenol Synthesis involves the production of natural vanillin from eugenol, which is extracted from clove oil. This method is preferred due to its affordability and ease of production. Ferulic Acid Synthesis, on the other hand, involves the extraction and synthesis of vanillin from ferulic acid, which is derived from rice bran or oats. This method offers a more sustainable and eco-friendly approach to producing natural vanillin. Both methods cater to the growing demand for natural and clean-label food ingredients.</p></p>
