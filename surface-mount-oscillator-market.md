<p><p>Surface Mount Oscillator (SMO) is a compact electronic component used for generating steady and accurate frequencies in various electronic devices. The SMO market consists of two main types; Surface Mounted Device (SMD) Oscillator and Surface Mount Technology (SMT) Oscillator. SMD Oscillators are small in size, designed for automated assembly using tape and reel packaging, and commonly found in consumer electronics. SMT Oscillators, on the other hand, are designed with advanced manufacturing techniques for improved performance and high-frequency applications, often used in telecom, aerospace, and defense sectors. Both types cater to the growing demand for compact and reliable frequency-generating components.</p></p>
