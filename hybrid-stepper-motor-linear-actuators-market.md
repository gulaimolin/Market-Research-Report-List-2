<p><p>Hybrid stepper motor linear actuators are commonly utilized in various industries for precise and controlled linear movements. These actuators come in different types, such as fixed shaft type, straight shaft type, and externally driven. The fixed shaft type has a stationary shaft that provides stability and support during linear movements. The straight shaft type has a straight and flexible shaft that allows for smooth and precise linear motion. On the other hand, externally driven actuators operate by combining a stepper motor with an external drive mechanism, providing increased torque and efficiency.</p></p>
