<p><p>Alternative dairy milk, also known as plant-based milk, is a non-dairy milk substitute made from plants such as soy, almond, rice, coconut, and oats. Consumers are increasingly seeking products with minimal additives, preservatives, and genetically modified organisms (GMOs). Manufacturers are responding to this trend by offering organic and natural alternatives, which are perceived to be healthier and environmentally sustainable.</p><p>Furthermore, the alternative dairy milk market is witnessing significant product launches and collaborations among key players to expand their product portfolios and reach a larger consumer base.
