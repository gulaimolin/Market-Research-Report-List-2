<p><p>Electrostatic Dissipative (ESD) flooring mats are specially designed mats that are used to control the static electricity generated on the surface of floors. They are commonly used in industries such as electronics manufacturing, telecommunications, healthcare, and aerospace, where the discharge of static electricity can cause damage to sensitive electronic components or equipment.</p><p>These mats work by providing a conductive surface that allows the static electricity to flow safely to the ground, preventing any buildup of static charge. They are typically made from a combination of materials like rubber or vinyl, infused with conductive elements such as carbon or copper.</p><p>The market for ESD flooring mats is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for electronics and the growing awareness about the importance of static control measures in various industries are key factors driving the market growth.
