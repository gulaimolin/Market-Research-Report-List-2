<p><p>The Draw Wire Encoder market consists of two main types: Absolute Draw Wire Encoder and Incremental Draw Wire Encoder. </p><p>An Absolute Draw Wire Encoder provides a unique digital code for each position of the wire, offering precise and accurate measurements. It doesn't require a reference point and retains its position even during power loss.</p><p>On the other hand, an Incremental Draw Wire Encoder generates a series of pulses as the wire moves, allowing for relative position measurements. It requires a reference point to determine the absolute position and may lose track of position during power loss.</p></p>
