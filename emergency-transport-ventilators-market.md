<p><p>Emergency transport ventilators are devices used to assist with breathing for patients during emergency transportation. There are three main types of these ventilators: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. Electronic ventilators use electricity to power the device and control the delivery of oxygen. Pneumatic ventilators use compressed gas to deliver oxygen to the patient's lungs. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine components of both electronic and pneumatic types to provide optimal control and oxygen delivery. These ventilators play a crucial role in ensuring patients receive adequate breathing support during emergency transportation.</p></p>
