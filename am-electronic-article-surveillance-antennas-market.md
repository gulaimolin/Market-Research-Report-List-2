<p><p>AM Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) antennas are used to prevent theft in retail stores. Single type market refers to individual antennas that are installed at the entrance and exit points, emitting and receiving signals to detect tags on items passing through. Pair type market involves a combination of two antennas, one emitting signals and the other receiving, creating an electromagnetic field between them. Any disruption, such as a stolen tag passing through, triggers an alarm. Both single and pair type antennas serve the same purpose but differ in terms of their design and installation configuration.</p></p>
