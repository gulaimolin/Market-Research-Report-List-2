<p><p>An infrared curing oven is a type of industrial oven that uses infrared radiation to cure or dry various materials such as paint, coatings, and adhesives. This heat then cures or dries the material in a more efficient and faster manner compared to traditional curing methods.</p><p>The future outlook of the infrared curing oven market is highly positive. The market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecasted period.
