<p><p>The electric juicing machines market can be categorized into three types - centrifugal, masticating, and others. Centrifugal juicers work by using sharp blades to shred fruits and vegetables, extracting juice through centrifugal force. Masticating juicers, on the other hand, use a slower grinding and chewing motion to extract juice, resulting in higher nutrient content and less oxidation. The "others" category includes various types of juicers such as citrus juicers and triturating juicers. Each type offers different features and benefits, catering to diverse consumer preferences in the juicing market.</p></p>
