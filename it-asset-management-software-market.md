<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>On-Premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IT Asset Management Software is available in two main types: Cloud-based and On-Premise. Cloud-based IT Asset Management Software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers benefits like reduced upfront costs, scalability, and ease of implementation. On the other hand, On-Premise IT Asset Management Software is installed directly on the user's servers and managed in-house. It provides greater control and security but requires higher upfront costs and technical expertise. Both types of software help organizations efficiently manage their IT assets and track their lifecycle from acquisition to disposal.</p></p>
