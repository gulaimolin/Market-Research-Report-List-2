<p><p>The Automated Teller Machine (ATM) market includes two main types: Cash Dispensers and Automated Deposit Terminals. Cash dispensers are typically used for cash withdrawals and balance inquiries, allowing users to access their bank accounts conveniently. On the other hand, Automated Deposit Terminals enable users to deposit checks, cash, or other forms of payment into their accounts. This type of ATM usually scans and verifies the deposited items before completing the transaction. Both Cash Dispensers and Automated Deposit Terminals provide self-service solutions and are widely used in banks, retail stores, and other locations for customer convenience.</p></p>
