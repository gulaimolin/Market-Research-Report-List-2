<p><p>The metal recycling market consists of various types, such as silver (Ag), gold (Au), and platinum group metals (PGMs). Silver recycling involves the collection and processing of scrap silver from various sources, which is then purified and reused. Similarly, gold recycling involves the extraction and refining of gold from old jewelry, electronics, and dental scrap for reuse. Platinum group metals recycling involves the recovery and reprocessing of platinum, palladium, and rhodium from catalytic converters, electronic waste, and industrial byproducts. These recycled metals play a crucial role in reducing the need for mining and conserving natural resources.</p></p>
