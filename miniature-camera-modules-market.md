<p><ul><li>CCD Imaging</li><li>CMOS Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The miniature camera modules market is categorized into two types - CCD imaging and CMOS imaging. CCD imaging refers to the use of charge-coupled devices, which convert light into electronic signals for producing high-quality images. CMOS imaging, on the other hand, utilizes complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology and offers advantages like lower power consumption and faster processing speed. Both types are widely used in various applications such as smartphones, automotive systems, and security cameras, offering users different options based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
