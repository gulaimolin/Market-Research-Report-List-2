<p><p>The Transmission & Distribution (T&D) Equipment market includes various types of equipment used in the electricity distribution process. Meters measure electrical usage for billing purposes. Inductors and capacitors are used for regulating voltage and power flow. Other T&D equipment includes line reactors, circuit breakers, and surge arresters. These equipment types play crucial roles in ensuring the safe and efficient transmission and distribution of electricity.</p></p>
