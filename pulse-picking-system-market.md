<p><p>The Pulse Picking System market comprises two main types of modulators: Electro-optic Modulator (EOM) and Acousto-optic Modulator (AOM). EOMs use the electric field to control the transmission of light, while AOMs utilize acoustic waves for the same purpose. Both modulators are widely used in laser systems to pick and control the output pulses. EOMs are preferred for their fast response time and excellent reliability, while AOMs offer the advantage of wide application range and high modulation speeds. These modulators play a crucial role in various industries, including telecommunications, scientific research, and laser manufacturing.</p></p>
