<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The radiator valves market is divided into three main types: manual radiator valves, thermostatic radiator valves, and traditional valves. Manual radiator valves allow manual control of the radiator's temperature by adjusting the flow of hot water. Thermostatic radiator valves automatically control the temperature by sensing the room's temperature and adjusting the flow accordingly. Traditional valves, on the other hand, are older types of valves that require manual adjustment but do not have the temperature-sensing capabilities of thermostatic valves. These different valve types cater to various consumer preferences and needs for efficient temperature control in homes or offices.</p></p>
