<p><p>Bioabsorbable surgical mesh is a type of medical device used in surgical procedures to provide support and reinforcement to weakened or damaged tissues. Unlike traditional surgical meshes, bioabsorbable meshes are designed to be gradually absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for a second surgery to remove the mesh.</p><p>The global bioabsorbable surgical mesh market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Factors driving this growth include the increasing prevalence of hernia and other soft tissue repairs, advancements in surgical techniques, the rising demand for minimally invasive procedures, and a growing aging population.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is also driven by the advantages of bioabsorbable mesh over non-absorbable mesh, such as reduced risk of infection, decreased post-operative pain, and eliminated long-term complications. The rising awareness about these benefits among healthcare professionals and patients is expected to further boost market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the bioabsorbable surgical mesh market include technological advancements in mesh materials, such as the development of composite meshes that offer enhanced strength and flexibility. Additionally, there is a growing focus on the use of bioactive coatings on the mesh to promote tissue regeneration and improve patient outcomes.</p><p>Furthermore, increasing research and development activities by key players to develop innovative bioabsorbable mesh products with improved biocompatibility and strength are expected to drive market growth.
