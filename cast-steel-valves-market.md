<p><p>The cast steel valves market includes various types of valves such as cast steel gate valves, cast steel globe valves, and cast steel check valves. Cast steel gate valves are designed to control fluid flow by opening or closing a gate mechanism. Cast steel globe valves regulate flow by moving a disc against the flow direction. Cast steel check valves enable one-way flow by allowing fluid to pass through only in one direction. These valves play a crucial role in controlling and regulating the flow of fluids in various industries.</p></p>
