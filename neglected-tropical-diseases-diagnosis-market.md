<p><p>Neglected tropical diseases (NTDs) diagnosis market consists of two major types: conventional method and molecular/modern method. The conventional method refers to the traditional diagnostic techniques used to identify NTDs, such as microscopic examination of blood, stool, or tissue samples. On the other hand, the molecular/modern method involves advanced technologies like polymerase chain reaction (PCR) or serological tests, enabling faster and more accurate detection of NTDs. These modern methods offer improved sensitivity and specificity, allowing for early diagnosis and better management of NTDs.</p></p>
