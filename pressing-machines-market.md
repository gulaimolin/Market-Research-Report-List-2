<p><p>The pressing machines market can be divided into three main types: manual pressing machines, semi-automatic pressing machines, and fully-automatic pressing machines. </p><p>Manual pressing machines require human operation for every step of the pressing process. Semi-automatic pressing machines involve a combination of human and machine operation, with some tasks automated to reduce manual effort. Fully-automatic pressing machines are designed to operate independently, with little to no human intervention required. These different types cater to various production needs, ranging from small-scale operations to large-scale industrial settings.</p></p>
