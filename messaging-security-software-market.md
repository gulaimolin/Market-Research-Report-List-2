<p><p>Messaging Security Software market includes two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed and managed directly on the user's own hardware and infrastructure. It offers greater control and customization but requires dedicated resources for maintenance and updates. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted and managed by third-party providers, enabling easy scalability and accessibility from any device or location. It offers the advantage of reduced costs and simplified management, as the provider handles updates and maintenance remotely. Both types aim to protect messaging systems from threats like spam, malware, and data breaches.</p></p>
