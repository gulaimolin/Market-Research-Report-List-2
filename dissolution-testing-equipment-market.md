<p><p>Dissolution testing equipment is used to determine the rate at which a drug substance is released from its dosage form. It is an essential step in the development and production of pharmaceutical products, as it ensures the drug's quality, safety, and efficacy by measuring its dissolution profile. The equipment consists of a dissolution vessel, paddle or basket, temperature control system, and a system to collect and analyze the released drug substance.</p><p>The global dissolution testing equipment market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by factors such as the increasing demand for pharmaceutical products, the need for quality control of drugs, and the stringent regulatory requirements for drug release testing. Automation also enables continuous monitoring of the dissolution process and real-time data analysis.</p><p>Another trend is the development of dissolution testing equipment with enhanced capabilities, such as the ability to simulate in vivo conditions accurately.
