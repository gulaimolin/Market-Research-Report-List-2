<p><p>Instrumentation fittings are used in various industries to connect and control instruments such as pressure gauges, flow meters, and sensors. Tube fittings are specifically designed for tubing systems and provide leak-free connections, including compression fittings and flare fittings. On the other hand, pipe fittings are used for piping systems and come in various shapes and sizes, including couplings, elbows, and tees. Both types of fittings are crucial for ensuring the reliability and accuracy of instrument measurements in industrial processes.</p></p>
