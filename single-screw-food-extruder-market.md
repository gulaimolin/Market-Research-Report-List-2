<p><p>The Single Screw Food Extruder market comprises two main types: Cold Extrusion Food Extruder and Hot Extrusion Food Extruder. Cold Extrusion Food Extruder machines are used to process food products at lower temperatures, typically below 100°C, to maintain the nutritional value and taste. On the other hand, Hot Extrusion Food Extruder machines operate at higher temperatures, usually above 100°C, and are suitable for manufacturing products that require cooking or baking processes. These two types cater to different food processing needs, ensuring flexibility and efficiency in the food industry.</p></p>
