<p><p>A galvanic oxygen sensor is a type of sensor used to measure the concentration of oxygen in a gas or liquid. When oxygen molecules come into contact with the sensing electrode, a chemical reaction occurs, generating an electrical current that is proportional to the concentration of oxygen present.</p><p>The galvanic oxygen sensor market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. <p><p>The Galvanic Oxygen Sensor Market includes two major types: Stress Control and Mass Flow Control. Stress Control refers to the ability of the sensor to work reliably under harsh conditions, such as extreme temperatures or pressure variations. This type of sensor is essential in industries where environmental conditions may affect the accuracy of oxygen measurement. On the other hand, Mass Flow Control refers to the ability of the sensor to accurately measure oxygen levels in gas streams with varying flow rates. This type of sensor is commonly used in applications where precise control and monitoring of oxygen levels is critical.</p></p>
