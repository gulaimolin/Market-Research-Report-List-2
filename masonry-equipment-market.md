<p><p>The masonry equipment market consists of various types of tools and equipment used in masonry construction. Masonry trowels are handheld tools with flat, pointed or rounded blades used for spreading, leveling, and shaping mortar or concrete. Masonry chisels are cutting tools with a sharp edge used for chipping or cutting bricks, stones, or concrete blocks. Masonry jointers are tools used to create or shape mortar joints in masonry structures. Masonry miscellaneous refers to other equipment including hammers, brushes, levels, and measuring tools used in masonry work. Other market segments may include specialized or innovative masonry equipment not covered under the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
