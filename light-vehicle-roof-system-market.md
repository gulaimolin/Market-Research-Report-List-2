Conventional sunroofs are fixed or tilting glass panels that can be opened for ventilation. Convertible roofs are retractable roofs that can be folded down, transforming a vehicle into an open-top car. Large sunroofs are panoramic glass roofs that cover a significant portion of the vehicle's roof, providing an open and spacious feel. These roof types offer different levels of openness and versatility to meet the preferences of consumers.</p></p>
