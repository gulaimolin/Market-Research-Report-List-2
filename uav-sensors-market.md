<p><p>The UAV sensors market comprises various types of sensors that are used in unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) for different functionalities. Distance sensors are utilized to measure the distance between the UAV and objects in its vicinity. Time of Flight (ToF) sensors measure the time taken by the emitted light to reflect back, providing depth perception. Thermal sensors detect and measure heat signatures for applications like search and rescue. Chemical sensors are capable of identifying and analyzing various substances in the environment. Orientation sensors provide data regarding the UAV's position and attitude. Lastly, the market also includes other types of sensors catering to different specific requirements.</p></p>
