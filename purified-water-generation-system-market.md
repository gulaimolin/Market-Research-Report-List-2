<p><ul><li>UV Based</li><li>RO Based</li><li>Gravity Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The purified water generation system market consists of three main types: UV-based, RO-based, and gravity-based systems. UV-based systems use ultraviolet radiation to kill bacteria and other microorganisms in water. RO-based systems employ reverse osmosis technology to remove impurities by forcing water through a semi-permeable membrane. Gravity-based systems rely on gravity and natural filtration processes to eliminate debris and contaminants from water. Each system offers its own advantages and is utilized in various settings depending on the purity requirements and availability of resources.</p></p>
