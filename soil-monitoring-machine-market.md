<p><p>Soil monitoring machines are divided into two main market types: hardware devices and software. Hardware devices refer to the physical components of the monitoring machine, such as sensors, probes, and data loggers, that are used to collect and measure soil-related parameters like moisture, temperature, pH levels, and nutrient content. On the other hand, the software market of soil monitoring machines includes the technologies and programs used to analyze, interpret, and visualize the data collected by the hardware devices. This software enables users to make informed decisions based on the data obtained from the hardware devices.</p></p>
