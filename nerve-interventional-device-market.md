<p><p>The Nerve Interventional Device market includes various types of devices that are used in the treatment of nerve-related disorders. These devices include microcatheters, which are thin tubes used to navigate through narrow blood vessels or nerves. Spring coils are used to embolize or block blood vessels. Brackets are used for the fixation of nerve tissues. Bolt Removal Bracket System is utilized in the removal of bolts used for fixation. Distal Access Catheter aids in accessing small peripheral blood vessels. Additionally, there are other devices available in the market that cater to specific nerve intervention procedures.</p></p>
