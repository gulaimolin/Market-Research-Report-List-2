<p><p>The anti-vibration mounts market consists of various types of mounts that are designed to reduce vibrations and dampen shock in different applications. Cylindrical mounts are cylindrical-shaped and provide isolation from vibrations in both horizontal and vertical directions. Bushing mounts are designed with rubber or elastomeric materials, providing flexibility and absorbing shocks. They are commonly used in engine systems. Conical mounts are cone-shaped and suitable for applications requiring compact and lightweight designs. They offer excellent isolation and damping properties, making them ideal for precision instruments and electronic devices.</p></p>
