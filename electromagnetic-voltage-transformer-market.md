Single Phase transformers are designed to handle a single waveform, usually found in residential and small commercial applications. On the other hand, Three Phase transformers are equipped to handle three waveforms, typically used in industrial and large commercial settings. Single Phase transformers have a simpler construction, lower cost, and lower power capacity compared to Three Phase transformers, which are more complex, pricier, and capable of handling higher power loads.</p></p>
