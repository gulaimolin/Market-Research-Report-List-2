<p><strong>The Subtitling and Transcription Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Muxed Subtitles</li><li>Burned Subtitles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Subtitling and transcription services encompass various market types, including muxed subtitles and burned subtitles. Muxed subtitles are integrated directly into the video file, allowing users to enable or disable them as needed. On the other hand, burned subtitles are permanently added to the video and cannot be turned off. Both services cater to different needs, with muxed subtitles offering flexibility for viewers while burned subtitles ensure subtitles are always present. These market types provide options for individuals and businesses seeking efficient and adaptable solutions for subtitling and transcription needs.</p></p>
