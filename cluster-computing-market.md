<p><p>Cluster computing refers to the use of multiple computers or nodes interconnected to work together as a single system to perform complex computational tasks. This technology allows organizations to handle massive amounts of data, perform complex calculations, and run intensive applications more efficiently and quickly than traditional single-server systems.</p><p>The Cluster Computing Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for high-performance computing in various industries such as banking, finance, healthcare, and research is driving the market growth. Cluster computing enables organizations to process large volumes of data in real-time and support advanced analytics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning applications. The rising adoption of these technologies across different sectors is fueling the demand for cluster computing solutions.</p><p>Furthermore, the emergence of cloud computing and the need for scalable and cost-effective computing solutions are boosting the market growth. Cloud-based cluster computing offers organizations the flexibility to scale their computing resources based on demand, avoiding the need for significant investments in infrastructure and hardware.</p><p>The market is also witnessing various technological advancements and trends.
