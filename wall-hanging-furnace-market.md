<p><p>The wall hanging furnace market consists of two main types: noncondensing type and condensing type. Noncondensing type wall hanging furnaces are traditional models that extract heat from the burned fuel and expel exhaust gases directly into the atmosphere. On the other hand, condensing type wall hanging furnaces use advanced technology to recover heat from the exhaust gases, resulting in better energy efficiency and lower emissions. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of consumers, offering options for both conventional and environmentally-conscious users.</p></p>
