<p><p>Nebulizers are devices used to deliver medicine directly into the lungs to treat respiratory conditions. The market for nebulizers consists of various types, including pneumatic nebulizers, ultrasonic nebulizers, mesh nebulizers, and others. Pneumatic nebulizers utilize compressed air to convert liquid medication into a fine mist. Ultrasonic nebulizers use high-frequency vibrations to create aerosol particles. Mesh nebulizers employ a mesh or membrane with tiny holes to generate a fine mist. Other types may include breath-actuated nebulizers or vibrating mesh nebulizers. These technologies offer different benefits and are used based on patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
