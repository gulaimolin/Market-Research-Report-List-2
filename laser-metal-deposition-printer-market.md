<p><strong>The Laser Metal Deposition Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Based</li><li>Wire Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Metal Deposition Printer market encompasses two main types: Powder Based and Wire Based systems. Powder Based printers use a laser to melt metal powder, layer by layer, to create the desired 3D object. This type offers versatility in material selection and is suitable for complex shapes. On the other hand, Wire Based printers use a laser to melt metal wire, creating the object through deposition. These printers are typically more cost-effective and suitable for larger-scale production. Both types showcase advanced laser technology and contribute to the growth of additive manufacturing.</p></p>
