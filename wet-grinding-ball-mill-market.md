<p><p>Wet grinding ball mill market is divided into two types: light ball mill and heavy ball mill. A light ball mill is mainly used for small-scale production, whereas a heavy ball mill is utilized for large-scale production. Light ball mills are generally designed with a horizontal cylindrical structure and are suitable for grinding light materials such as silica. On the other hand, heavy ball mills have a robust and durable design and are ideal for grinding heavy and hard materials such as ores and minerals.</p></p>
