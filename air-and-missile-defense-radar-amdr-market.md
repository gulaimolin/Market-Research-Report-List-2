Ground-Based AMDR systems are utilized for defense against airborne threats across land territories. Naval-Based AMDR systems are installed on naval vessels to identify and counter aerial and missile threats on the sea. Airborne-Based AMDR refers to radar systems mounted on aircraft for airborne surveillance and defense purposes. Each type of AMDR system serves a specific operational requirement in detecting and intercepting air and missile threats.</p></p>
