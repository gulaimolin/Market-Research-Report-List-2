<p><p>Twin-screw extruders are used in various industries for processing materials. They are available in two types: co-rotating and counter rotating. In co-rotating twin-screw extruders, both screws rotate in the same direction, working together to process the material. On the other hand, in counter rotating twin-screw extruders, the screws rotate in opposite directions, offering enhanced mixing capabilities. Both types have their unique advantages and are suitable for different applications depending on the material properties, desired outputs, and processing requirements.</p></p>
