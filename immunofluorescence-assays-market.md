<p><p>The Immunofluorescence Assays Market comprises three major types: Antibodies, Kits and Reagents, and Labeling Dyes. Antibodies are used as primary and secondary detection tools to identify specific proteins in samples. Kits and Reagents include pre-packaged sets that contain all necessary components for immunofluorescence assays, such as blocking agents and mounting media. Labeling dyes are fluorescent probes that directly bind to target molecules, enabling their visualization under a fluorescence microscope. These market segments play crucial roles in facilitating accurate and efficient immunofluorescence assays in research, diagnostics, and drug discovery.</p></p>
