<p><p>The healthcare waste management system market is segmented into three types: incineration, autoclaving, and others. Incineration involves the burning of healthcare waste materials at high temperatures, reducing them to ashes and gases. Autoclaving, on the other hand, uses high-pressure steam to sterilize the waste before disposal. The "others" category includes methods like microwave treatment, chemical treatment, and landfilling. These different types of waste management systems ensure the safe and effective disposal of healthcare waste, preventing the spread of infections and minimizing environmental hazards.</p></p>
