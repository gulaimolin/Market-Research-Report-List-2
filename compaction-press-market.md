<p><p>The compaction press market can be classified into two types: roller compactors and radial compactors. Roller compactors are machines that utilize two or more rotating drums to compact material by subjecting it to pressure. This type of compactor is commonly used in road construction and landfill applications. On the other hand, radial compactors are designed with a rotating plate that exerts pressure on the material radially. They are often utilized in landscaping, creating foundations, and compacting soil in narrow areas such as trenches or around pipes. Both types of compactors play a vital role in various industries where compaction is required.</p></p>
