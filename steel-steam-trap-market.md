<p><strong>The Steel Steam Trap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermodynamic</li><li>Mechanical</li><li>Thermostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel steam trap market consists of three types: thermodynamic, mechanical, and thermostatic. Thermodynamic traps have a disc or disc assembly that opens and closes depending on the flow of steam and condensate. Mechanical traps use a float or bucket system to control the flow of condensate. Thermostatic traps have a temperature-sensitive element that opens and closes the valve based on the condensate's temperature. Each type offers different benefits and is suitable for various applications in industries like power generation and oil & gas.</p></p>
