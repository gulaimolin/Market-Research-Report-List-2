<p><p>Exoskeleton robots refer to wearable robotic devices that help enhance human performance, assist with physical tasks, or aid in rehabilitation. There are three types of exoskeleton robots based on their coverage on the human body: lower, upper, and full body. Lower body exoskeletons focus on assisting lower limb movements, offering support for walking or standing. Upper body exoskeletons provide assistance for upper limb movements, such as lifting heavy objects. Full body exoskeletons cover both the upper and lower body, offering comprehensive support and assistance for a wide range of physical activities.</p></p>
