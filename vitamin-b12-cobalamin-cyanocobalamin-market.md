Vitamin B12 injections are often prescribed to individuals with severe deficiencies or absorption problems. The convenience and effectiveness of injections have made them a preferred choice for some patients.</p><p>Furthermore, there is a growing interest in the development of alternative sources of Vitamin B12, especially for the vegan population.
