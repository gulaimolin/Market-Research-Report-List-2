<p><p>Room pressure monitors are devices used to measure and monitor the pressure differentials between different spaces within a building. There are two types of room pressure monitors: fixed and portable. Fixed room pressure monitors are permanently installed within a specific space and provide continuous monitoring. They are typically used in critical environments such as hospitals or pharmaceutical laboratories. On the other hand, portable room pressure monitors are compact and easily transportable. They are suitable for temporary monitoring purposes, such as checking pressure differentials during construction or renovation projects.</p></p>
