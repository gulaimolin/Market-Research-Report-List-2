<p><p>The Leaf Remover Market consists of two main types: Mechanical Leaf Remover and Pneumatic Leaf Remover. Mechanical Leaf Removers are designed with rotating blades or brushes that help remove leaves from the ground by cutting or sweeping them away. They are suitable for smaller areas and are manually operated. On the other hand, Pneumatic Leaf Removers operate by generating suction or blowing air to collect and remove leaves. They are more efficient for larger areas as they can cover a wider radius and require less manual effort.</p></p>
