<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Spray Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Spray Dryer</li><li>Stream Spray Dryer</li><li>Centrifuge Spray Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray dryers are used in various industries to convert liquid or slurry into powders or granules. The pressure spray dryer operates by pumping the liquid into the nozzle where it is atomized and then dried, suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Stream spray dryer works by spraying the liquid into a heated chamber where the moisture evaporates instantaneously, enabling fast drying. Centrifuge spray dryer involves atomizing the liquid into a rotating drum, creating a fine mist that dries rapidly due to the high centrifugal force. These different types cater to specific drying requirements in different industries.</p></p>
