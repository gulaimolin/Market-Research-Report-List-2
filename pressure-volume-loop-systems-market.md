<p><p>In the Pressure-Volume Loop Systems market, there are different types of systems available to measure various parameters related to cardiac function. These include systems for measuring Ejection Fraction (the percentage of blood pumped out of the heart with each beat), Max and Min Ventricular dP/dt (the rate of pressure change), Cardiac Output (the volume of blood pumped by the heart per minute), Contractility Index (the heart's ability to contract), Elastance (the heart's stiffness), Stroke Volume (the volume of blood ejected with each heartbeat), Pre-load Recruitable Stroke Work (the work done by the heart per unit of pre-load). These systems help in evaluating and diagnosing cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
