<p><p>Linear Encoders : Linear encoders are used to measure linear position and displacement in various industrial applications. They consist of a scale and a sensor that detects movement and provides corresponding digital output. Linear encoders are commonly used in CNC machines, coordinate measuring machines, and other high-precision machinery.</p><p>Rotary Encoders : Rotary encoders are used to measure angular position and rotation in a wide range of applications. They consist of a disc or ring with encoded markings and a sensor that detects the changes in the markings as the disc rotates. Rotary encoders are widely used in robotics, motor control systems, industrial machinery, and other applications requiring precise angular position feedback.</p></p>
