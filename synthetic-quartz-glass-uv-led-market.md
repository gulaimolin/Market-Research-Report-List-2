<p><p>The synthetic quartz glass UV-LED market includes three types: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LED emits long-wave ultraviolet light, suitable for applications like polymerization, lithography, and counterfeit detection. UV-B LED emits medium-wave ultraviolet light, used for medical purposes like phototherapy and sterilization. UV-C LED emits short-wave ultraviolet light, known for its germicidal properties, used in water purification, air sterilization, and surface disinfection. Each type of UV-LED serves specific purposes in various industries, offering efficient and effective solutions for different applications requiring ultraviolet light.</p></p>
