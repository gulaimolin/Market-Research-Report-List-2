<p><p>Wall panel systems have two main types: external and internal. External wall panel systems are designed to be used in outdoor applications, providing insulation and protection against weather elements. These panels are typically made of durable materials and are installed on the exterior of buildings. On the other hand, internal wall panel systems are used for interior applications. They are primarily used for aesthetic purposes, enhancing the appearance of walls while also providing sound insulation and improving energy efficiency. Internal wall panel systems can be made of various materials, such as wood, gypsum, or metal.</p></p>
