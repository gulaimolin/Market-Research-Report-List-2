<p><p>A Commercial AI Security Camera is a type of surveillance camera integrated with artificial intelligence (AI) technology that can analyze and interpret the captured video footage in real-time. This technology enables cameras to adapt to different lighting conditions, object orientations, and angles, minimizing false alarms and improving overall performance.</p><p>Additionally, the advent of edge computing, wherein AI algorithms are processed locally on the cameras themselves rather than sending the video footage to a central server, is gaining traction in the market. Edge computing reduces latency, enhances data security, and allows for real-time decision making, making it an attractive option for commercial applications.</p><p>Overall, the Commercial AI Security Camera market is witnessing steady growth, fueled by the increasing need for advanced surveillance solutions.
