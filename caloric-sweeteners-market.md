<p><p>Caloric sweeteners are widely used in the food and beverage industry as alternatives to sugar. It is commonly used in sugar-free or reduced sugar products due to its ability to provide sweetness without adding calories. Mannitol is another sugar alcohol used as a caloric sweetener, primarily in chewing gums and confectionery.
