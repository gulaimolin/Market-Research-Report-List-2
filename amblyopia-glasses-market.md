<p><p>The Amblyopia Glasses market can be categorized into different types based on the cause of amblyopia. Strabismic Amblyopia stems from misaligned eyes, which results in poor brain-eye coordination. Other types of amblyopia include Anisometropic Amblyopia and Stimulus Deprivation Amblyopia. Understanding these categories helps in developing specific types of glasses suitable for each condition, targeting the unique needs of individuals with amblyopia.</p></p>
