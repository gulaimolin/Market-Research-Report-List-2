<p><strong>The Portable Electric Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convector Heaters</li><li>Oil-filled Heaters</li><li>Halogen Heaters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable electric heater market consists of several types of heaters. Convector heaters are designed to quickly heat up a room using a heating element and a fan to circulate warm air. Oil-filled heaters use a heating element to heat oil inside the heater, which then radiates heat into the room. Halogen heaters employ halogen elements to generate heat through infrared radiation. Other types of electric heaters may include radiant heaters or ceramic heaters, which use heating elements to emit heat directly. These different types cater to various consumer preferences and heating requirements.</p></p>
