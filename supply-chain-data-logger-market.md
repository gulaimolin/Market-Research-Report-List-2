<p><p>The supply chain data logger market includes two main types of loggers: disposable data loggers and reusable data loggers. Disposable data loggers are designed for single-use applications and are typically lower in cost. They are ideal for tracking temperature, humidity, and other environmental factors during transportation or storage. Reusable data loggers, on the other hand, are built for multiple uses and offer more advanced features like data analysis and wireless connectivity. They are suitable for long-term monitoring and provide a cost-effective solution for companies with frequent supply chain operations.</p></p>
