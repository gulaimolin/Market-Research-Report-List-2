<p><p>Medical Transcription Software (MTS) market types primarily include web-based MTS, cloud-based MTS, and on-premise MTS. Web-based MTS is an internet-dependent software that offers accessibility from any device with an internet connection. Cloud-based MTS operates on remote servers, facilitating data storage, management, and accessibility from anywhere. On-premise MTS is hosted locally, enabling healthcare providers to have complete control and customization over the software's infrastructure. All these market types serve the purpose of enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of medical documentation by converting voice recordings into written reports.</p></p>
