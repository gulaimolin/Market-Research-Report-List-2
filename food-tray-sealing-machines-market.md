<p><p>Food tray sealing machines are used to seal food trays to maintain product freshness and ensure food safety. There are three types of machines: Manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operators to manually place the tray and seal it. Semi-automatic machines partially automate the process by automatically placing the tray, while operators manually seal it. Fully automatic machines automate the entire process, including tray placement and sealing, requiring minimal operator involvement. These machines cater to different production scales and offer varying levels of operator control and efficiency.</p></p>
