<p><p>The Residential HVAC Equipment Market consists of two main types: Direct Expansion Systems and Central Air Conditioning Systems. Direct Expansion Systems use refrigerants to cool air directly within individual rooms or areas, commonly found in window air conditioners and split systems. Central Air Conditioning Systems, on the other hand, use a central unit to cool the entire home through a network of ducts. These systems are typically found in larger homes or commercial buildings and provide more comprehensive cooling capabilities.</p></p>
