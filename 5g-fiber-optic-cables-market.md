<p><p>The 5G Fiber Optic Cables market consists of two main types: Single-Mode Cable and Multi-Mode Cable. Single-Mode Cable is designed for long-distance transmission, allowing a single beam of light to travel through a narrow core. It is suitable for high-speed and high-capacity data transfer over long distances. On the other hand, Multi-Mode Cable has a larger core diameter and is used for shorter distances, providing multiple light beams to travel simultaneously. It is ideal for local area networks and short-range communication applications.</p></p>
