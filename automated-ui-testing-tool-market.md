<p><p>Automated UI Testing Tool market can be categorized into two types: On-Premise and Web Based. On-Premise tools are installed on the user's local server and are managed and maintained by the user. They provide complete control over the testing environment but require higher upfront costs and regular maintenance. Web-Based tools, on the other hand, are hosted on the cloud and accessed through web browsers. They offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness but may have limited control over the testing environment. Each type has its own advantages and considerations, allowing users to choose the most suitable option based on their specific needs.</p></p>
