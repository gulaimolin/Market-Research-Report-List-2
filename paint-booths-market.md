<p><ul><li>Open Type Paint Booths</li><li>Closed Type Paint Booths</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paint booths are classified into two types: open type and closed type. Open type paint booths are designed to provide ventilation while painting materials such as vehicles or furniture. They have certain open spaces to allow fresh air to enter and provide proper airflow for the paint process. Closed type paint booths, on the other hand, are enclosed structures with filtration systems. They efficiently control airflow and prevent contamination from external sources, ensuring a clean and controlled environment for painting operations.</p></p>
