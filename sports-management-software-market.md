<p><p>Sports Management Software is available in two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-premise software is installed locally, on the organization's own servers or computers. It requires physical infrastructure and IT maintenance, suited for organizations with strict data security regulations. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, accessed through the internet. It offers more flexibility, scalability, and accessibility, making it popular among organizations of all sizes. Cloud-based software eliminates the need for infrastructure maintenance and allows real-time collaboration from anywhere with an internet connection.</p></p>
