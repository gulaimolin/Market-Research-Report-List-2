<p><p>Collaborative robot reduction gears, specifically the RV reducer and harmonic reducer, are types of components used in collaborative robots to transmit torque and reduce rotational speed. The RV reducer employsa cycloid disc mechanism, which allows for compact designs, high precision, and increased torque capacity. On the other hand, the harmonic reducer employs flexible s-shaped wave generators to achieve highly efficient, compact, and low-backlash motion control. Both types of reduction gears play a crucial role in enhancing the overall performance and functionality of collaborative robots.</p></p>
