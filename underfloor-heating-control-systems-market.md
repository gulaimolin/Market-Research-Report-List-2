<p><strong>The Underfloor Heating Control Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfloor heating control systems market is divided into two types: electric and hydraulic systems. Electric underfloor heating control systems rely on electric currents to generate heat. They use a network of electric heating cables or mats installed beneath the floor to warm the space. On the other hand, hydraulic underfloor heating control systems utilize heated water or a liquid solution to provide warmth. These systems consist of pipes that circulate the heated liquid throughout the floor, effectively heating the room above.</p></p>
