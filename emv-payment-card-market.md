<p><p>The EMV payment card market consists of three types: contactless cards, contact cards, and dual-interface cards. Contactless cards enable users to make payments by simply tapping or waving the card near a payment terminal. Contact cards require physical contact with the terminal, where the card is inserted and held until the transaction is complete. Dual-interface cards combine both contactless and contact card capabilities, allowing users to choose the desired payment method. These different card types provide users with various payment options for easier and more convenient transactions.</p></p>
