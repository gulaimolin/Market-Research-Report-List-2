<p><strong>The Fogging Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gloss Method</li><li>Gravimetric Method</li><li>Haze Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fogging tester market types include the gloss method, gravimetric method, and haze method. The gloss method is used to measure the amount of residue deposited on glass surfaces, such as car windshields. The gravimetric method determines the weight of the fogging film that settles on a surface within a specific time frame. On the other hand, the haze method measures the optical quality of transparent materials by quantifying the amount of light scattered or transmitted through them. These market types assist manufacturers in ensuring product quality and meeting industry regulations.</p></p>
