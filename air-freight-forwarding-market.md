<p><ul><li>Full Charter</li><li>Split Charter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the air freight forwarding market, there are two types of markets: full charter and split charter. Full charter refers to the entire aircraft being chartered by a single entity to transport its cargo. This option is typically chosen when there is a large volume of goods to be transported. On the other hand, split charter involves multiple entities sharing the same aircraft to transport their respective cargoes. This option is usually preferred when individual entities have smaller quantities of goods to be transported.</p></p>
