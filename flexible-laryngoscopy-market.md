<p><ul><li>Single-Use Flexible Laryngoscopy</li><li>Reusable Flexible Laryngoscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible laryngoscopy market consists of two types: single-use flexible laryngoscopy and reusable flexible laryngoscopy. Single-use flexible laryngoscopy refers to the one-time disposable devices used for examining the larynx and vocal cords. These devices offer convenience, minimize infection risks, and eliminate the need for sterilization. On the other hand, reusable flexible laryngoscopy involves the utilization of sterilizable devices that can be used multiple times. It offers cost-effectiveness but requires proper cleaning and sterilization procedures after each use to maintain safety and avoid cross-contamination.</p></p>
