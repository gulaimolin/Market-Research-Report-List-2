<p><p>A LED light bar is a form of lighting fixture that utilizes light-emitting diodes (LEDs) as its source of illumination. LED light bars are commonly used in automotive, marine, industrial, and off-road applications due to their energy efficiency, durability, and high light output.</p><p>The LED light bar market has experienced significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory in the forecast period.
