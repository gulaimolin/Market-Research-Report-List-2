<p><p>The electric tool LED light market is divided into three types: flood light, spot light, and area light. Spotlights, on the other hand, emit a concentrated beam of light, ideal for focusing on specific details or objects. Lastly, area lights offer a combination of flood and spot lights, providing a broader coverage while also allowing for precise illumination. These different types cater to various needs and preferences of consumers in the electric tool LED light market.</p></p>
