<p><p>Sweet potato fries can be categorized into two main market types: strip sweet potato fries and irregular sweet potato fries. Strip sweet potato fries are uniform in shape and size, cut into thin and even strips. They are favored for their uniform texture and appearance, making them more suitable for commercial or fast-food establishments. On the other hand, irregular sweet potato fries have a non-uniform shape and size, appealing to customers who prefer a more rustic and natural appearance. Irregular fries are often associated with homemade or specialty food establishments.</p></p>
