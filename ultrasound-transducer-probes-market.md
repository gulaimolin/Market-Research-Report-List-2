<p><p>The ultrasound transducer probes market includes various types of probes used in ultrasound imaging. Linear type probes are commonly used for musculoskeletal and vascular examinations due to their high resolution. Convex type probes are used for abdominal and obstetric imaging as they provide a wider field of view. Phased array type probes are used for cardiac imaging as they offer better penetration and visualization of cardiac structures. Endocavitary type probes are designed for internal examinations, particularly in gynecology and urology. Other types of probes may include specialty probes for specific applications such as ophthalmic or transesophageal imaging.</p></p>
