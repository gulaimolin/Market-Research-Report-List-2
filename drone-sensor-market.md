<p><p>The drone sensor market consists of various types of sensors that enable drones to gather critical data. Inertial sensors like accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetometers, and tilt sensors measure movements and orientation. Image sensors such as infrared, thermal, multispectral, and 3D sensors capture visual information. Speed and distance sensors like radar, LiDAR, and proximity sensors determine the drone's position relative to objects. Position sensors like GPS and GNSS are used for accurate positioning. Pressure sensors measure atmospheric pressure, while current sensors monitor power consumption. Additionally, there are ultrasonic, light, and altimeter sensors available in the market.</p></p>
