<p><p>The Bone Growth Stimulation Devices Market consists of various types of therapies. Inductive Coupling Therapy uses electromagnetic fields to generate a low-level electric current, stimulating bone healing. Capacitive Coupling Therapy utilizes an electrical charge, promoting the movement of charged particles within bone cells. Combined Therapy combines both inductive and capacitive coupling, allowing for a comprehensive approach to bone growth stimulation. These therapies help accelerate the healing process, particularly in cases of delayed union or nonunion fractures.</p></p>
