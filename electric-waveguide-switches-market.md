<p><ul><li>Rectangular Waveguide Switch</li><li>Double Ridge Waveguide Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric waveguide switches are devices used in telecommunications and microwave systems to control the routing of signals. The rectangular waveguide switch is designed with a rectangular cross-section, enabling the transmission of electromagnetic waves in a specific direction. On the other hand, the double ridge waveguide switch features a double ridged structure, allowing for the transition between different waveguide modes. Both types of switches play a crucial role in ensuring efficient signal transmission and are widely used in various industries, including aerospace, defense, and telecommunications.</p></p>
