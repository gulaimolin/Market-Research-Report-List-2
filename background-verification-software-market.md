<p><p>Background Verification Software market includes two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted and operated on the vendor's servers, allowing users to access it through the internet. This type offers scalability, flexibility, and minimal upfront costs. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed and operated on the user's own servers and infrastructure.
