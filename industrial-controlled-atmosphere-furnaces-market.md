<p><p>Industrial controlled atmosphere furnaces are specialized heat treatment equipment used in industries for processes such as annealing, brazing, sintering, and tempering. <p><p>Industrial controlled atmosphere furnaces are used for various heat treatment processes in different industries. The market is divided into two types: horizontal type and vertical type. Horizontal type furnaces have a horizontal chamber where the material to be treated is placed. They are generally used for batch processes and are suitable for large and heavy components. On the other hand, vertical type furnaces have a vertical design, making them more suitable for continuous processes and smaller components. Both types offer controlled atmosphere conditions for precise and efficient heat treatment.</p></p>
