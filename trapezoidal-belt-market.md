<p><p>A trapezoidal belt, also known as a V-belt or a wedge belt, is a type of power transmission belt that has a trapezoidal cross-section. Trapezoidal belts are designed to handle high-speed operations and heavy loads, providing reliable power transmission and reducing the risk of slippage.</p><p>The trapezoidal belt market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
