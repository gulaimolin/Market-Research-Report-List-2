<p><p>A micro tillage machine, also known as a mini tiller or mini cultivator, is a small agricultural machine used for mechanical tillage and soil cultivation. It is primarily used in small farms or gardens where larger farming equipment is not suitable.</p><p>Micro tillage machines are designed to loosen and cultivate the soil, preparing it for planting or seeding. They are compact, lightweight, and easy to maneuver, making them ideal for small-scale gardening or farming. These machines typically feature rotating blades or tines that loosen and churn the soil, allowing for better aeration and nutrient absorption.</p><p>The micro tillage machine market is expected to experience significant growth in the forecast period.
