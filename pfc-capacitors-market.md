<p><p>The PFC (Power Factor Correction) capacitors market can be categorized into two types based on voltage requirements: Low Voltage and Medium Voltage. Low Voltage PFC capacitors are designed to operate in electrical systems where the voltage level is below 1 kV. These capacitors are commonly used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications to improve power factor and reduce energy consumption. On the other hand, Medium Voltage PFC capacitors are specifically designed for electrical systems with voltage levels ranging from 1 kV to 36 kV. These capacitors are typically used in utility substations and industrial facilities to improve power quality and ensure efficient energy transmission.</p></p>
