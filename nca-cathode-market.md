<p><p>The NCA Cathode market includes two types - Conventional NCA Cathode and High Pressure Solid NCA Cathode. Conventional NCA Cathode refers to the traditional form of cathodes used in lithium-ion batteries, composed of layered materials known as nickel-cobalt-aluminum (NCA) with a graphite anode. On the other hand, High Pressure Solid NCA Cathode is an innovative variation, where the cathode is made into a solid state, enhancing the stability and performance of the battery. Both types play crucial roles in the development of high-energy density, long-lasting lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
